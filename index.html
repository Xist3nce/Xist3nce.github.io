About Me

Hello! My name is Trent, otherwise known as Sleepy Entity. I am an American indie game developer working to create some fun experiences, and maybe one day work on the game of my dreams! I am currently capable of programming in C#, C++, and Python. I am formally trained in using Autodesk Maya and Zbrush. I've done most of my work in Unity3D, however I have worked a bit in Unreal Engine 4 and godot as well. If you've read this far you're likely looking for my games catalogue or are looking to contact me. Feel free to contact me via any of the social media listed in the footer (I'm most active on twitter), or use the contact form and I will get back to you as soon as possible. Thanks for reading, and have a wonderful day!
